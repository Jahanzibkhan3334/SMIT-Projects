Olympic Game Events Of Athletics

The Summer Olympics has 48 athletics events, including 23 for men, 23 for women, and 2 mixed events. The events are similar between men and women, but men compete in the 50 km race walk.

World Athletics Championships

The World Athletics Championships has 49 events, including 24 for men, 24 for women, and one mixed team relay.

Men's meets

A men's meet can have as many as 25 events.
