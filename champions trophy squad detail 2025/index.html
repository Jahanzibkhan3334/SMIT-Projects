The ICC Champions Trophy 2025

The ICC Champions Trophy 2025 is set to be an exciting cricket tournament, taking place from February 19 to March 9, 2025, in Pakistan. This will be the ninth edition of the competition and marks the first major ICC event hosted in Pakistan since 1996. The matches will be held in three iconic venues: National Stadium in Karachi, Gaddafi Stadium in Lahore, and Rawalpindi Cricket Stadium

The ICC Champions Trophy 2025, scheduled from February 19 to March 9 in Pakistan, marks the return of this iconic tournament after eight years, bringing together the top eight ODI teams in the cricketing world. Hosted across historic venues like Lahore, Karachi, and Rawalpindi, the event promises intense battles and dramatic moments, with global stars like Babar Azam, Rohit Sharma, and Pat Cummins leading their nations. The tournament’s format ensures high-stakes clashes from the very first game, including the much-anticipated India-Pakistan rivalry, which is sure to capture worldwide attention. This event isn't just about cricket—it's a celebration of the sport's global spirit, history, and future
